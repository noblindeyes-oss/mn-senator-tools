An advanced EMT or paramedic who lives within the coverage zone and responds immediately in a personal vehicle with a jump bag (AED, airway kit, IV supplies) while the ambulance is en route. The sprint medic provides first-contact care in the critical window before the ambulance arrives, compressing effective response time by 4–10 minutes on average. Minnesota's Sprint Medic Pilot Program, funded through the 2023 EMS legislation, deployed this model in Otter Tail and Grant counties.
