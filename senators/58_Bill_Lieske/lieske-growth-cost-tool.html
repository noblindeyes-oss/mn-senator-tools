Key Terms

SAC (Sewer Access Charge)
A one-time fee per new connection to the municipal sewer system. In cities outside the Metropolitan Urban Service Area (MUSA), the city sets and retains this fee. Inside MUSA, the Metropolitan Council charges its own SAC; cities may add a local charge on top.
WAC (Water Access Charge)
A one-time fee per new connection to the municipal water system, set by the city.
MUSA (Metropolitan Urban Service Area)
The geographic boundary within which Metropolitan Council Environmental Services (MCES) provides wastewater treatment. Cities inside MUSA connect to the regional system and pay Met Council SAC. Many SD-58 communities with their own treatment systems are outside MUSA. Verify your city's status with the Metropolitan Council or Rice/Scott County planning office.
Trunk Main
A large-diameter water main that carries supply from the source (well or tower) to distribution lines within a development.
Lift Station
A pumping station that moves wastewater from a lower to higher elevation when gravity flow is not possible.
