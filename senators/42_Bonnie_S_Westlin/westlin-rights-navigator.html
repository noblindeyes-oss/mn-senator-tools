Minnesota Foster Youth Rights Navigator

"Know Your Rights. Document What Happened. File Your Complaint."

Senator Westlin's SF3154 established a Foster Youth Bill of Rights in Minnesota -- a detailed framework of protections for children and young adults in the foster care system. This tool was built to put those rights directly in the hands of the youth they were written to protect.

Privacy: Nothing you enter here is sent anywhere. This tool works entirely in your browser. Your answers are not stored on any server.

Minnesota law gives foster youth specific rights -- to monthly visits from your caseworker, to be part of your placement plan, to contact your siblings, and more.
