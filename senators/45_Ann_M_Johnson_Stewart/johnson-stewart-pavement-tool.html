The Minnesota Municipal State Aid Street (MSAS) program allocates funding to cities with populations over 5,000 based on population, city street mileage, and construction needs. Cities submit a needs assessment through MnDOT's SANEEDS3 system. This module displays your MSAS-eligible segments and generates a plain-language narrative for submission.
