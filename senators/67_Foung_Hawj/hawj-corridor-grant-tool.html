What Did Construction Cost Your Business?

If MnDOT's Arcade Street or East 7th Street construction has hurt your business, you may be eligible for a grant of up to $5,000. This tool helps you document your revenue loss, check your eligibility, and generate a ready-to-submit application — no accountant needed.

Senator Hawj's SF2520 secured the grants. This tool helps you claim one.

Senator Foung Hawj's SF2520 (2025) created this grant program after the East Side Area Business Association documented that approximately 150 small businesses — many of them immigrant-owned — faced revenue losses when MnDOT began reconstructing Arcade Street and East 7th Street in spring 2025. The $250,000 appropriation funds grants of up to $5,000 per business, distributed by ESNDC.
