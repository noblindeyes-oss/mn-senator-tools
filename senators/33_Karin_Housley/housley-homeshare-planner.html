Minnesota Nice HomeShare

"Is HomeSharing Right for You?" — A Step-by-Step Guide

Washington County has thousands of seniors who own their homes but struggle with rising costs — and thousands of adults who can't find housing they can afford in communities they want to live in. Sen. Housley's Minnesota Nice HomeShare bill is designed to connect them. This tool helps you figure out if HomeSharing could work for your household, what you'd need to prepare, and how to describe yourself to a potential match.

Important: Sen. Housley's SF2085 is a pilot program — it is not yet law and not yet funded. This tool is designed to help Washington County residents understand whether HomeSharing could work for them, and to be ready when the program launches. Nothing in this tool constitutes an application to any program.

Sen. Housley's SF2085 would create the Minnesota Nice HomeShare program — connecting Washington County seniors who have spare rooms with adults who need affordable housing nearby. This tool was built to give those residents something concrete to work with while the program finds its funding.
