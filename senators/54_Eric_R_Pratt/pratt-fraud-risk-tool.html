Senator Pratt spent 30 years identifying fraud vulnerabilities in banking — at U.S. Bank and Target — before bringing that focus to the Minnesota Senate. This tool puts that methodology in the hands of every small business in Scott County. Complete the assessment in 15 minutes. Act on the results immediately.

Occupational fraud is fraud committed by employees against their own employer — not cybercrime from outsiders. It includes billing schemes, check tampering, payroll fraud, expense reimbursement abuse, and skimming. ACFE research consistently finds that small organizations with fewer than 100 employees suffer median losses of $141,000 per case because they have fewer segregation-of-duty controls than large companies.

Scott County's growth corridor. The Hwy 169 and I-35 corridors are attracting new employers and entrepreneurs every year. Fast growth means new employees, new systems, and new fraud exposure. This tool was built for that moment.
