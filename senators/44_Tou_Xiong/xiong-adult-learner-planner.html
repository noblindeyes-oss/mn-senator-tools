About This Tool

Senator Tou Xiong's SF2341 put $65 million behind adult basic education in Minnesota. This tool was built to help the people in his district actually find and use those programs. He graduated from Tartan High School — the same ISD 622 district that runs the North Saint Paul-Maplewood ABE program this tool highlights. The path he took is the path this tool maps.

Senator Xiong chairs the Senate State and Local Government Committee. He was born to Hmong refugees from Laos, earned his B.A. in Economics from St. Cloud State University and his J.D. from William Mitchell College of Law (now Mitchell Hamline). He has worked at Minnesota Legal Aid, the Public Health Law Center, and served on the Ramsey County Library Board of Directors.

A resource for SD-44 communities and adult learners across Minnesota. Built with AI to demonstrate what technology can do for the people Senator Xiong's legislation serves. Data reflects Minnesota ABE programs and test costs as of 2025. All information should be verified with the relevant institution before making decisions.
