Seller provided a document explaining how contracts for deed work, including the 120-day cancellation provision and the risk of losing all paid amounts if unable to complete payments 559A.03, subd. 1(a)(4)
Cancellation Right
Buyer informed of the right to cancel the purchase agreement within 10 calendar days of receiving disclosures 559A.04, subd. 1
Language Access
If any part of negotiations occurred in a language other than English, were all disclosures provided in that same language? 559A.03, subd. 2
Churning Indicator
Has this seller previously offered you or someone you know a contract for deed on this same property that was later terminated? 559A.01, subd. 3 / 559A.04, subd. 3

Your Rights Under Minnesota Statute 559A

Under 559A.04, you may cancel the purchase agreement within 10 calendar days of receiving required disclosures.

Under 559A.05, you have two years from contract execution to seek rescission and damages for material disclosure violations.

If the seller terminates this contract within 48 months, any down payment amount exceeding 10% of the purchase price must be returned within 180 days.

Contact: Minnesota Attorney General (mn.gov/ag) or HOME Line (612-728-5767) if you believe your rights have been violated.
