Minnesota Local Election Administration Compliance Tracker

Every Deadline. Every Statute. Every Step.

Senator Koran serves as Ranking Member on the Minnesota Senate Elections Committee — and his district's exurban counties are managing growing voter rolls with the same small election administration offices they've always had. This tool was built to help those officials keep up.

Minnesota local election officials — county auditors, city clerks, and township clerks — manage dozens of overlapping statutory deadlines across every election cycle. This tool translates Minnesota election law (Chapters 203B, 204B, 204C, and 206.89) into a date-specific compliance calendar, phase-by-phase checklists, and a post-election audit calculator.
