Scholarship Expansion: Old Law vs. SF 5435

County mineral-tax scholarships were limited to traditional degree programs. SF 5435 opens eligibility to accredited trade and vocational programs.

Old Law

  • 4-year university programs
  • Community college transfer tracks
  • Traditional academic scholarships only
  • Trade/vocational programs excluded

SF 5435

  • All previous programs retained
  • Welding programs now eligible
  • Equipment operation programs
  • Electrical trade programs
  • Pipefitting certification
  • HVAC technician training
  • Heavy machinery maintenance

An estimated 55-60% of young workers trained in SD-3 leave the region within 5 years. The top pull factors: Twin Cities wages, Duluth amenities, limited housing stock on the Range.
