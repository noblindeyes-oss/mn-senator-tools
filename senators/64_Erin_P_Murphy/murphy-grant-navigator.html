About this program: The Capitol Area Economic Development Grant Program was established in the 2025 Minnesota special session capital package to support businesses in the St. Paul Capitol Area. Grants of $50,000 to $150,000 are available for equipment purchases, facade improvements, and other capital investments. For current program details, eligibility rules, and application deadlines, contact the administering state agency directly. This tool is a planning aid, not an official application portal.
