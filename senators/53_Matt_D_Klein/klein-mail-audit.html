Minnesota Business Filing Fraud Prevention Act — SF1734

Is This Mailing Legitimate?

Senator Klein's SF1734 created enforceable rules for a specific scam: mailers designed to look like government notices that charge small business owners for filings they don't need. This tool was built to make those rules usable by the people the law was written to protect.

If you received a mailing you're not sure about, start with the Fraud Identification Checker. If your business sends filing-related mailings, use the Sender Compliance Audit to verify your materials before mailing. Both modes generate a printable report.

⚠ Important Notice — BOI Filing

FinCEN's Beneficial Ownership Information (BOI) reporting rule was struck down by federal courts in 2025. Any mailing demanding BOI compliance fees or urgent action is almost certainly fraudulent. There is currently no federal BOI reporting requirement in effect. Do not pay and do not provide your information to any third party claiming to offer BOI filing services.
