Prompts to Consider
  1. 01 What did you learn about how your community makes decisions?
  2. 02 Which of the five competency domains challenged you most, and why?
  3. 03 What surprised you about your civics project — what went differently than planned?
  4. 04 How has your understanding of media and information changed?
  5. 05 Describe a moment when you had to listen seriously to a view you disagreed with.
  6. 06 What does democratic engagement mean to you now versus at the start?
  7. 07 How did your project address a real community need? What evidence do you have?
  8. 08 What would you do differently, and what will you carry forward as a citizen?
